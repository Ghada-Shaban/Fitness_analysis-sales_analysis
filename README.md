# üìà Fitness analysis(Sales analysis)
A comprehensive dashboard analyzing revenue, profit, costs, returns, and customer behavior across products, regions,
and time periods to identify trends, optimize strategies, and support data-driven business decisions.

---


# üìã Project Requirements
The analysis was performed  using data imported from multiple Excel files containing sales, products, customers, etc.

- **Data Cleaning:** Removed duplicates, corrected inconsistencies, and handled missing values.

- **Data Integration:** Combined multiple Excel datasets.

- **Analytical Readiness:** Prepared metrics for revenue, profit, cost, average order value (AOV), returns, and customer behavior.

- **Dashboarding:** Created interactive dashboards to explore trends by product, category, price segment, region, business type, and customer activity.

---


## ‚ùóProblem Statement
The company has experienced significant variations in revenue, profit, costs, and customer activity over the reported period. Performance differs across products, categories, regions, and customer segments. Seasonal and monthly fluctuations are evident, as well as differences in product pricing and returns. A clear understanding of these patterns is required to support data-driven decision-making.

---



## üßê Key Business Questions

- Which products and categories generate**the highest revenue and profit**, and which may require marketing focus or quality assessment?
- How are revenue and profit distributed across regions and countries, and which **areas show potential for increased market presence or distribution**?
- What are the trends in customer behavior, including **active customers, customers with no orders, and customers who return products**?
- How do **revenue and profit fluctuate on a monthly and quarterly basis**, and which periods show the greatest growth opportunities?
- What is the **return rate and its impact** on revenue and profit, and which products or months have the highest returns?
- How do products perform across different **price categories, and which categories achieve higher margins** that could be leveraged?
- How can repeat **order frequency and average order value (AOV)** be improved among existing customers?
